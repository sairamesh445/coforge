About Coforge

Coforge is a global digital services and solutions provider, focusing on delivering transformative services to clients across various industries.
Headquartered in India, it has a significant presence in North America, Europe, Asia, and Australia.
The company specializes in digital process automation, artificial intelligence, and cloud computing.
Coforge partners with leading technology providers to enhance its service offerings.
It is known for its strong client relationships and innovative approach to solving complex business challenges.

History Of Coforge


Coforge, originally founded as part of NIIT in 1981, initially focused on IT education and training before evolving into NIIT Technologies in the late 1990s to provide IT services and solutions.
The company went public in 2004 and expanded globally, establishing a strong presence in North America, Europe, Asia, and Australia.
Strategic acquisitions and investments in emerging technologies like AI, cloud computing, and digital process automation have marked its growth.
In August 2020, the company rebranded to Coforge, reflecting its transformation into a global digital services leader.
Coforge continues to enhance its capabilities and market reach through innovation and strategic partnerships.
